Last Chance

Overview

Last Chance is a post-apocalyptic roleplay game based on Joueur du Grenier's RPG serie Les Suvivants.

Background

What year is it ? We do not know... The epidemic is raging and decimating the largest cities. First, the capital, then the periphery, little by little... And finally, the little tourist town of Berezino, where the army tried to evacuate the population toward Siberia. Trains and buses were mobilized, but the end of the convoy turned out to be a real fiasco; everyone wanted to escape as quickly as possible, then the riot broke out. The bus 1138 attempted to leave the city vefore its departure order, but was instantly stopped by the military forces. The passengers were transferred urgently to the main hospital, the least injured got out a few hours or days later, but the others, in critical conditions, were abandoned.

At the same time, a small group of marauders, looting the city, meet the bus survivors. Mistrust prevails between the 13 suvivors, but they need to help each other to get out of this nightmare.

Game System

Characters

Each player draws a character from the 12 in the group (one of them cannot be played) and plays it until the end of the adventure or his character's death. In the second case, he will draw a new character until there is no longer character to play, meaning the escape has failed. The character sheets are avaible here.

A character sheet has seven statistics : Fight, Shoot, Strength, Agitily, Intelligence, Perception and Stress, bewteen 0 and 100, and 3 to 6 special abilities (unique or not). Every action is associated to a statistic and the player has to possibility to use an ability to help the group.
