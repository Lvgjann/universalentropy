Fire Nebula

Outter Rim

A nebula is an interstellar cloud of dust, hydrogen, helium and other ionized gases.

Like books and pages. Pages are dust, but when we gather them, it creates something. A cloud of pages, like a nebula. A literary nebula. You will find here my drafts, novels and poems. As I am French, this website will be in French, except my novels which will be translated in English soon.
