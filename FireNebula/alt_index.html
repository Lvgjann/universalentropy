About me


Who am I ?

My name is Florian Crampe - Fi Skirata, I am a 19 student in computer science at the IUT Informatique de Bordeaux. I am also a musicien, composer, writer and rolist.

... as a musician

I've been practicing music for 10 years as singer, guitarist, bassist, pianist and drummer. I can also play any other plucked string instruments and some wind instrument.


... as a writer and rolist

I write poems and novels, mostly science fiction, but also thriller
