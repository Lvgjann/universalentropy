Rapture's Sinking

Plot

The Atlas revolution is raging in Rapture and riots are popping up all over Andrew Ryan's utopian city. No side really wins, the dead accumulate, the ADAM evaporates over the battles, but nothing advances.
Many try to flee the underwater city, but few succeed without having to resist the idealism of Ryan or the fanaticism of Atlas. Geographically, Andrew Ryan's tower and Fontaine's Industries are at the two opposite of Rapture, and between them, the anarchy.
